The SAMURAI Framework

Sake (鮭) - Understand the Problem
Trace back to the root to understand the situation and define the aspiration.
Aji (鯵) - Gather Insights
Fresh and relevant insights through data collection and initial hypotheses.
Maki (巻) - Roll Out Ideas
Brainstorm and prioritize ideas wrapped seamlessly.
Uni (海胆) - Dive Deep
Dive deep into challenges with detailed analysis and validation.
Rāmen (ラーメン) - Interconnect Solutions
Blend multiple strategies together for an interconnected solution.
Ahi (鮪) - Test and Iterate
Test solutions in a controlled environment and iterate for improvement.
Ikigai (生き甲斐) - Align with Purpose
Ensure solutions align with the core purpose and gain stakeholder buy-in.
